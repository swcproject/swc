About SWC

SWC (Smart Waste Classifier) is an innovative AI-powered solution that combines machine learning with Arduino Uno technology to revolutionize waste classification. Developed by Swapnil Pandey, this cutting-edge system helps users identify and segregate waste intelligently into three categories: Recyclable, Non-Recyclable, and Organic Waste.

Our Vision

Our vision is to transform India into a nation where every citizen is educated about waste management and empowered to segregate waste effectively. We believe in fostering "Sustainable Development" through technology, creating a cleaner tomorrow for future generations.

Camera Access Required

To use the camera for real-time classification:

  1. Click "Allow" when prompted for camera permission
  2. If blocked, click the camera icon in your browser's address bar
  3. Enable camera access and refresh the page
  4. Alternatively, use "Upload Image" to classify photos

Frequently Asked Questions

Camera not working?
If camera access is blocked, you can still use the model by uploading images! Click "Upload Image" and select a photo from your device. The AI will classify it instantly. For camera access, check your browser permissions and allow camera use.
Should I use this model for waste segregation at home?
Absolutely! This model is designed to help households segregate waste correctly into Recyclable, Non-Recyclable, and Organic categories. Use it as a learning tool to understand proper waste classification and contribute to environmental sustainability.
How accurate is this model?
Our model achieves high accuracy through training on over 22,000 diverse waste images across all three categories. While highly reliable, we recommend using it as a helpful guide alongside your knowledge of local waste management guidelines.
Does it work on mobile devices?
Yes! Our responsive design ensures the classifier works seamlessly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. Both camera and image upload work perfectly on mobile devices.
